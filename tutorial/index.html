How to use the digital Hack Week World

The SUSE Hack Week is not only the time to innovate and grow but also to meet different people inside the company. This time we want to bring the socializing to you in another format: A digital SUSE Hack Week World based on workadventu.re!

Navigate the world

  • use the arrow keys to navigate around the world
  • use the shift key to run
  • enter the proximity of another player to start a voicechat
  • use the meeting rooms to talk to bigger groups
  • explore the maps and find hidden treasures!
